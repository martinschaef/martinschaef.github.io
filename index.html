Martin Schäf

       

Dr. Martin Schäf is an Applied Scientist at Amazon Web Services. Prior to that, he worked as a Senior Computer Scientist at SRI International in Menlo Park, CA, and at the United Nations University in Macau. He received his PhD from University of Freiburg in 2011, and his MS degree in computer science from Saarland University in 2006. His research interests include static analysis, software verification, fault localization, and test automation.
