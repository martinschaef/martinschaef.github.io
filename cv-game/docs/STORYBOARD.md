# Career Quest: Level Storyboard

## Level 1: Saarbrücken (The Tutorial)
- **Environment:** A 16-bit cozy house in a traditional coal-mining city.
- **Goal:** Navigate the childhood home, speak with **Parents** to initialize the "Life Quest," and dodge **3 Siblings** moving in random patterns. Find the "Abitur Scroll" to unlock the front door.
- **Combat:** No damage taken. Martin uses the "Basic Keyboard Swing" to clear piles of "Abitur Prep Books" and "Clutter Boxes" blocking the exit.
- **Career Stage:** Youth & High School. The foundation before the academic journey begins.

---

## Level 2: Freiburg (The PhD Quest)
- **Environment:** Misty, dense Black Forest university campus with gothic stone architecture.
- **Goal:** Find **Andreas Podelski** in the Tower of Logic. Collect "Stamina Pizza" from roommates **Stephan and Even**. Navigate the "State-Space Explosion" maze to defend the thesis.
- **Combat:** "Casual Combat" begins. Battle "Infinite Loops" (spinning enemies) and "Null Pointer Wraiths." 
- **Key Mechanic:** Acquire the "Static Analysis Shield" to block incoming red-pen projectiles.
- **Career Stage:** PhD Candidate at Albert-Ludwigs-Universität Freiburg. Research focusing on program verification and static analysis.

---

## Level 3: Macau (The UN & Casino Duality)
- **Environment:** A split map—one half is the sleek, minimalist United Nations University; the other is the neon-soaked, flickering lights of the Cotai Strip.
- **Goal:** Perform "Context Switching" tasks. Help **The Diplomats** with software security while moonlighting for **The Pit Bosses** to optimize casino systems.
- **Combat:** Enemies are "Security Vulnerabilities" and "Legacy Code Blobs." Use "Bounty Hunter Logic" to clear the screen of inefficient code.
- **Career Stage:** Postdoctoral Fellow at UNU-IIST and developer for the gaming industry.

---

## Level 4: San Francisco / Menlo Park (The Research Lab)
- **Environment:** The fog-covered hills of the Bay Area, featuring the legendary "SRI International" tech-campus.
- **Goal:** Collect "Peer-Reviewed Citations" scattered across the hilly terrain. Meet with fellow researchers to survive the "Conference Deadline" countdown timer.
- **Combat:** Battle "Vague Specifications" and "Reviewer #2" (a flying red pen). Martin's keyboard is now upgraded with "Formal Methods" range.
- **Career Stage:** Computer Scientist at SRI International, focusing on automated reasoning and bug finding.

---

## Level 5: New York City (The Cloud Citadel)
- **Environment:** Rain-slicked Manhattan streets with glowing green AWS skyscrapers reaching into the clouds.
- **Goal:** Meet **Byron Cook** at the base of the AWS Tower. Deploy "Formal Verification at Scale" to the cloud nodes. Navigate through high-speed yellow cab traffic to reach the final server room.
- **Combat:** Final Boss: "The Global Outage." Use the "Senior Principal Engineer Armor" and "Cloud Keyboard" to verify the logic of the entire city.
- **Career Stage:** Senior Principal Engineer at Amazon Web Services (AWS).
